THEORIES
───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Theory 1: Mirrored Geometry + Shared Texture Conflict
  • Left track has scale.x = -1 (mirroring)
  • WebGL may not handle shared textures on mirrored geometry
  • Why it fails: GPU texture coordinate system gets confused
  • Test: Remove mirroring, see if both tracks render

Theory 2: Three.js Material/Texture Lifecycle Bug
  • First material correctly binds texture
  • Subsequent materials fail to upload texture to GPU
  • Why bodies/weapons work: Different shader compilation?
  • Test: Use MeshBasicMaterial (simpler shader)

Theory 3: WebGL Context Texture Slot Limit
  • GPU has limited texture slots
  • Bodies + weapons + tracks use 3 slots
  • Multiple materials sharing textures may exceed limits
  • Test: Create unique texture instances per material
